Famous For

Jagannath Temple is famous for being one of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Jagannath. It is renowned for the grand **Rath Yatra**, which attracts millions of devotees every year. The temple is also known for its unique wooden idols of Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra. Additionally, it is famous for its Mahaprasad and magnificent Kalinga-style architecture.


Jagannath Temple is one of the most sacred Hindu temples and is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu. It is one of the four important **Char Dham** pilgrimage sites in India and attracts millions of devotees every year.


The temple is world-famous for the **Rath Yatra**, during which the deities of Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra are taken in beautifully decorated giant wooden chariots through the streets of Puri. This festival is celebrated with great devotion and enthusiasm.


The temple is also known for its magnificent Kalinga-style architecture and its sacred **Mahaprasad**, which is prepared daily in one of the largest temple kitchens in the world. Its rich history, traditions, and spiritual significance make it a major cultural and religious landmark in India.
