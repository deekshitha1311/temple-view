Timings of Jagannath Temple

Jagannath Temple is generally open for devotees from around **5:00 AM until about 11:00 PM**, although the exact timings may vary depending on daily rituals and festivals. The temple follows a strict schedule of पूजा (sevas) throughout the day, so darshan may be briefly paused during these ceremonies. Visitors are advised to arrive early in the morning or in the evening for a smoother experience. It is always best to check the day's schedule before visiting, especially during major festivals.
